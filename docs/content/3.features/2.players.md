## Online Players

The online players section shows all players that are currently online on the server. For each player, you can view:

- **Name & UUID**: The player's Minecraft username and unique identifier
- **World**: The current world the player is in
- **IP Address**: The player's connection IP
- **Health & Hunger**: Current health and hunger levels
- **Gamemode**: The player's current gamemode (Survival, Creative, Adventure, Spectator)
- **Playtime**: Total time the player has been online
- **Operator Status**: Whether the player has operator privileges

### Player Actions

Right-click on any online player to open the actions menu:

- **Change Gamemode**: Switch between Survival, Creative, Adventure, or Spectator
- **Teleport**: Send the player to coordinates, to another player, to spawn, or to a different world
- **Inventory**: View and edit what the player is carrying - move, give, or clear items
- **Profile**: Look at the player's stats and details
- **Whisper**: Send a private message that only that player sees
- **Kick**: Remove the player from the server with an optional reason
- **Mute / Temp-ban**: Silence a player or ban them for a set amount of time

::alert{type="info" icon="lucide:info"}
Some actions depend on your server software. Anything that isn't supported on your platform simply won't appear in the menu.
::

## Whitelisted Players

The whitelist section allows you to manage which players are allowed to join your server when the whitelist is enabled.

### Managing the Whitelist

- **Enable/Disable**: Toggle the whitelist on or off using the switch at the top
- **Add Player**: Enter a player's username to add them to the whitelist
- **Remove Player**: Click the remove button next to any player to revoke their access

::alert{type="info" icon="lucide:info"}
Players added to the whitelist don't need to be online. VoxelDash will look up their UUID automatically.
::

## Banned Players

The banned players section displays all players who have been banned from your server. Each entry shows:

- **Player Name & UUID**: Identifying information
- **Ban Reason**: Why the player was banned
- **Banned By**: Who issued the ban
- **Ban Date**: When the ban was applied

### Managing Bans

- **Ban Player**: Add a new ban with a custom reason
- **Unban Player**: Remove a ban to allow the player to rejoin

## Operators

Manage server operators (admins) who have access to administrative commands:

- **Add Operator**: Grant operator privileges to a player
- **Remove Operator**: Revoke operator status
